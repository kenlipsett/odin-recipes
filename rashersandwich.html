Rasher Sandwich

Juicy Rasher Sandwich With Butter and Ketchup

Ingredients
  • White Bread
  • 3 thick cut smoked rashers
  • Real Butter
  • Heinz Ketchup
Steps
  1. Heat pan, fry the shit out of the rashers until starting to go black
  2. Butter one slice of bread and spread ketchup on the other
  3. Put the rashers onto the buttered slice of bread and slap on the other slice so ketchup is in contact with the rashers
  4. Cut in half
